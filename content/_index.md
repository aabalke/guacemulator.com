# {{< image src="favicon.svg" alt="guac logo" loading="lazy" >}} guac

guac is a gb, gbc, gba and nds emulator for windows, mac and linux. It aims to provide a fast and accurate emulation in golang, accomplishing this through the first-of-its-kind jit compiler, and 3d rasterizer. It is currently a work in progress; however, most games boot.
